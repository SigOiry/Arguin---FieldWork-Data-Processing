Atmospheric Correction

During the week of the fieldwork, ASD measurment have been acquired at the different location of the meadow visited by the team (Figure 1). These measurment covers a large range of target, from bare sediment to seagrasses and polychaete reefs. On the 27th of March, nowing that a Sentinel-2 acquisition was planned, special measurement over bright and stable targets (White and Dry Sand) were acquired in order to test the accuracy of the atmospheric correction applied to Sentinel-2 imagery (Sen2Cor).
